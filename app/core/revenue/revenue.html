What happened to my classifieds revenue?

Classifieds revenue for local media has plummeted for over a decade - in fact, the “classifieds - buy & sell” section doesn't exist any longer for most local media properties.

Back when classifieds revenue began declining, old school software providers introduced expensive and inadequate products attempting to fill the void for media. These products turned classifieds into cost centers by charging pricey monthly fees for low-quality, low volume, and unengaging content.

Back to basics

At Antengo, we believe in returning classifieds to the profit centers they once were for local media.
